history

wood movers tree service

premium tree service

Beginning as an urban logging company in 2005, Wood Movers has since transitioned to a tree service provider, with tree service now comprising over 90% of our work. The company is owned and managed by a graduate forester with over 40 years experience in removing trees. It is a member of the BBB and TCIA (Tree Care Industry Association). It's goal is to provide the homeowner with premium tree service at a less than premium price.
